WORK EXPERIENCE

Internship in Bosch Security Systems

Part of studying in my third year at university was to find an internship where I could work on my degree and develop my skills. After a few applications, I was invited for an interview at Bosch, whose office is located in Eindhoven, Torenallee 49. The interview included a technical part, which I was nervous about, but it wasn't long before I was notified that I had been selected to be part of the company. The assignment I dealt with during the period was focused on solving the problem of the product compliance manager and his workflow. As a client, he required me to compose the user interface of the application, which would be web-based and have multiple functionalities to cover his requirements. Initially, I had to come up with a design and then I had to come up with a software architecture that I could use and that was compatible with the backend. This was also part of the research, as besides the implementation, I was also evaluated on my findings relayed at the end of the internship. I chose to work on the assignment using React and Redux as I had experience with them, but wanted to further develop my skills in this area. Eventually I reached the MVP of the application and the client was more than happy.
